Our 3-Step Enrolment Process

Getting started is simple. We're here to guide you every step of the way.

01

15-Minute Phone Call

We start with a brief phone call to understand your situation, answer your initial questions, and see if the Arrowsmith Program might be a good fit. This is completely free and no-obligation.

02

45-Minute Consultation

If you'd like to learn more, we'll schedule a deeper consultation. We'll discuss learning history, challenges, and goals in detail. You can ask all your questions and get a clear picture of how the program works.

03

Trial Days in School

Experience the program firsthand with trial days at our school. This gives you and your child the chance to see what a typical day looks like and meet our teachers before making any commitment.
