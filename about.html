The A1 Student Arrowsmith Advantage

What makes our approach different and effective.

Established International Program

Arrowsmith has over 40 years of research and development, helping thousands of students worldwide.

Neuroplasticity

The brain strengthens and forms new neural pathways to improve its performance. This is the science behind our approach.

We Target Core Issues

By addressing the underlying cognitive deficit, students see lasting improvements to their learning abilities.

Scientific Research

Evidence-based research and brain imaging studies prove the effectiveness of the Arrowsmith approach.

Positive Structured Learning

Development of a growth mindset results in academic achievement, personal growth and social benefits.

Real-time Reporting

Stay informed with regular updates on your child's progress and cognitive development.
